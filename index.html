My brief CV

This is Yiming Sun,

an undergraduate at University of Science and Technology of China (USTC)

  • I am a senior year student majoring in Condensed Matter Physics and minoring in Computer Science.
  • Currently, I am a member of Imaging Quantum Materials Lab, led by Prof. Zhenyu Wang (USTC) and a visiting student at Prof. Kin Fai Mak and Prof. Jie Shan's group at Cornell University. My research topic is mainly about combining computer science (e.g. machine learning) and experimental techniques (e.g. ARPES, STM) to investigate the scientific mystery of correlated bulk materials and tunable quantum phases in two-dimensional structures. We also develop advanced numerical computation and computer vision approaches for simulating the experimental behavior (e.g. quasiparticle interference).
  • I am pursuing the further study in groundbreaking advance of application towards numerical and mathematical understanding of our world with computer science and physics!
